BAG REQUIREMNETS

01
Solarpanel
  • The bag design should include a dedicated pouch with a clear window panel for integrating a solar panel. This pouch should be strategically positioned on the exterior of the bag for easy access to sunlight.
  • The pouch material should be weatherproof, protecting the solar panel from rain, dust, and other environmental elements to ensure efficient charging in various conditions.
02
Flexible Umbrella Holder:
  • Elastic or adjustable straps with velcro closures for securing umbrellas of different sizes and styles to the exterior of the bag.
  • Reinforced stitching and durable materials to withstand the weight and movement of the umbrella, providing reliable support during travel in rainy conditions.
03
Secret Pouch for GPS:
  • Concealed compartment within the bag's interior or exterior for securely housing a GPS tracker, providing discreet tracking of the bag's location.
  • Easy access zipper or magnetic closure for quick retrieval of the GPS tracker when needed, without compromising security.
04
Velcro Attachment for Skateboard:
  • Velcro straps or loops on the exterior of the bag designed to securely hold a skateboard in place during travel.
  • Reinforced stitching and durable materials to withstand the weight and movement of the skateboard, ensuring stability and preventing damage to the bag.

PRODUCT REQUIREMNETS

01
Solarpanel
  • It should have dimensions of approximately 10 x 20 inches, striking a balance between surface area for charging and portability for travel.
  • Constructed from robust, weather-resistant materials to withstand outdoor conditions and ensure longevity.
  • Compatible with a wide range of devices, including smartphones, tablets, and other gadgets commonly used during travel.
02
Umbrella
  • A sturdy frame made of materials like fiberglass or metal to provide stability and withstand gusts of wind.
  • Folding mechanism for easy storage and portability, allowing users to carry the umbrella in bags or backpacks.
  • Canopy size sufficient to provide coverage for one or more individuals, offering protection from rain and sunlight.
03
GPS Tracker
  • Long-lasting battery life to support extended tracking periods without frequent recharging, ideal for long-distance travel.
  • Compact and lightweight design for discreet integration into bags or compartments without adding bulk or weight.
  • The GPS tracker should provide accurate real-time location tracking, ensuring users can locate their belongings with precision.
04
Skateboard
  • Designed for smooth and effortless maneuverability, allowing users to navigate crowded streets and sidewalks with ease.
  • Compact and lightweight design for easy carrying when not in use, without compromising on performance.
05
Biometric lock
  • Advanced biometric technology for reliable and secure locking mechanisms, ensuring only authorized access to belongings.
  • Durable construction to withstand tampering attempts and ensure the integrity of the locking system.
  • Compatible with various types of bags and compartments, offering flexible integration options for different travel accessories.
06
Powerbank
  • Rapid charging capability to quickly recharge devices, minimizing downtime and keeping users connected.
  • Equipped with multiple USB ports to charge multiple devices simultaneously, catering to the diverse needs of travelers.
